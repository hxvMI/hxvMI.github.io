Chocolate Brownie

This simple, fudgy brownies recipe might be the best you have ever tried. Fattening but worth it! These brownies can be frozen after baking.

Time Expectations

Stages Amounts of Time
Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 45 minutes
Cool Time: none
Total Time: 1 hour and 5 minutes

Ingredients

Amounts Ingredients
3 cup White Sugar
1 1/4 cup All-Purpose Flour
2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
7 Eggs
1 1/4 ounces Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
1 teaspoon Salt
1 pound Chopped Walnuts

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Line one 9x9-inch baking pan with foil and spray with cooking spray.
  3. In a saucepan over medium-heat, melt butter.
  4. Stir in sugar until dissolved. Remove mixture from heat.
  5. Gradually pour melted butter into beaten eggs, whisking constantly, until mixture is fully incorporated and stir in vanilla.
  6. Sift flour, cocoa, and salt together.
  7. Add flour mixture to butter mixture, mixing until combined.
  8. Stir in walnuts and spread batter into the prepared pan.
  9. Bake in preheated oven until brownies are set.
