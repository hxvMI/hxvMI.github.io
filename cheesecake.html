Cheesecake

This delectable dessert features a buttery graham cracker crust cradling a smooth and creamy cream cheese filling. Baked to perfection, the cheesecake emerges with a golden hue, enticing aromas, and a heavenly texture.

Time Expectations

Stages Amounts of Time
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 1 hour
Cool Time: 3 hours
Total Time: 4 hours and 15 minutes

Ingredients

Amounts Ingredients
1 tablespoon Melted Butter
1 cup Sour Cream
1/4 cup All-Purpose Flour
1 tablespoon Vanilla Extract
2/3 cups Milk
8 ounces Cream Cheese
4 Large Eggs
18 Graham Crackers
1 teaspoon Finely Grated Orange Zest
1 teaspoon Finely Grated Lemon Zest

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter together in a bowl until evenly moistened.
  3. Press crumb mixture into the bottom and about 1/2 inch up the sides of the springform pan.
  4. Whisk sour cream, flour, and vanilla extract together in a bowl.
  5. Stir cream cheese and sugar together with a wooden spoon in a separate bowl until evenly incorporated, 3 to 5 minutes
  6. Add milk and whisk until just combined.
  7. Stir in lemon zest, orange zest, and sour cream mixture; whisk until just incorporated.
  8. Pour mixture into prepared springform pan.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until the edges have nicely puffed and the surface of the cheesecake is firm except for a small spot in the center that will jiggle when the pan is gently shaken, about 1 hour.
  10. When the cheesecake is done, turn off the oven and let it cool in the oven for 3 to 4 hours.
