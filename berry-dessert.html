Berry Dessert

Indulge in the vibrant burst of flavors with our delightful Berry Dessert. This luscious treat features a medley of fresh, juicy berries, perfectly harmonized to create a symphony of sweetness.

Time Expectations

Stages Amounts of Time
Prep Time: 25 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Cool Time: 8 hours
Total Time: 8 hours and 50 minutes

Ingredients

Amounts Ingredients
8 ounces Seedless Raspberry Jam
1 pound Strawberries
12 ounces Raspberries
12 ounces Blueberries
1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
16 ounces Cream Cheese
1 cup Confectioners' Sugar
1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
7 ounces package Crisp Lady Fingers

Directions

  1. To begin, warm the raspberry jam in the microwave.
  2. Toss it with the berries and let it sit while you prepare the rest of the trifle.
  3. Beat the heavy cream until stiff peaks form, then set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the softened cream cheese and Confectioners’ sugar.
  5. Beat until smooth and creamy, then beat in the vanilla and add in a third of the whipped cream.
  6. Beat until smooth and then add the remaining cream.
  7. Fold with a rubber spatula until evenly combined.
  8. Top with a third of the berry mixture, followed by a third of the cream with cream cheese mixture on top.
  9. Let the trifle chill for at least 8 hours, then top with some fresh berries and a sprig of fresh mint, if desired.
